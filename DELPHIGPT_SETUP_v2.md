# DelphiGPT Professional Setup Guide

## Overview

DelphiGPT is a professional AI-powered carbon intelligence assistant integrated into the CO2 Simulation WebApp. It provides industry-grade emissions analysis, optimization strategies, and regulatory compliance guidance based on your facility's actual data.

## Professional Features

- **Comprehensive Data Analysis**: AI-powered insights using your complete facility emission profile
- **Industry-Grade Recommendations**: Professional optimization strategies with quantified ROI
- **Equipment Performance Analysis**: Detailed breakdown of high-emitting equipment with improvement potential
- **Fuel Strategy Assessment**: Cost-effective fuel switching analysis with implementation timelines
- **Regulatory Compliance Guidance**: Current regulatory requirements based on your emission levels
- **Professional Interface**: Clean, industry-standard design optimized for professional use

## AI Engine Configuration

### ✅ OpenAI GPT-4o Mini (Currently Active)

Your system is configured with OpenAI GPT-4o mini for optimal performance and cost efficiency.

**Current Configuration**:

- **Model**: GPT-4o mini (cost-optimized, high-performance)

- **Response Quality**: Professional, data-driven analysis
- **Cost**: ~$0.005-0.015 per query (90% cost reduction vs GPT-4)

## Usage Guide

### Accessing DelphiGPT

1. Navigate to the **Reporting Page**
2. Scroll to the **DelphiGPT Carbon Intelligence Assistant** section
3. Review your facility overview metrics displayed in the interface

### Asking Questions

- **Direct Input**: Type specific questions about your facility
- **Quick Actions**: Use pre-configured analysis buttons
- **Follow-up**: Build on previous responses for deeper analysis

### Professional Query Examples

- "Provide a comprehensive optimization strategy for reducing our facility's CO₂ emissions by 20%"
- "Analyze the ROI and implementation timeline for switching our diesel equipment to natural gas"
- "What are the regulatory compliance requirements for our current emission levels?"
- "Compare the cost-effectiveness of different fuel switching scenarios for our top emitting equipment"
- "Develop a 3-year emission reduction roadmap with specific milestones and targets"
